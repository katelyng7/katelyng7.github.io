Hi, I'm Katelyn Gan! 👋

I am an undergraduate student at [MIT](https://web.mit.edu/), double majoring in [Mathematics](https://math.mit.edu/) and [Computer Science](https://www.eecs.mit.edu/research/computer-science/). 

I am currently an Undergraduate Researcher at [MIT CSAIL](https://www.csail.mit.edu/node/2873) advised by Prof. [Kaiming He](https://people.csail.mit.edu/kaiming/), where I focus on training and optimizing Vision Transformers (ViT) on Google Cloud TPU clusters. Previously, I conducted research in computer vision for exoplanet detection and investigated game-theoretic behavior in combinatorics.
